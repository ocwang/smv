The Idea

Strategic Motion is a personalized, interactive video system specialized for corporate training. Through dynamic, engaging video content, Strategic Motion offers a corporate solution for effective, scalable training.

  Seamless, Dynamic Videos Customized For Each Learner

All trainings are personalized for each unique learner and dynamically adapt to fit the learner's needs, introducing content where it is most relevant.

  Increased User Engagement and Higher Transfer Rates

Instructors can employ the use of learner questions and assessment questions to engage learners, leading to more engagement and increased content retention rates.

  Real-time Analytics and Performance Data

The SMV system offers analytics and performance metrics including real-time statistics on user engagement, transfer rates, and learning assessments. Administrators can view performance data to identify common pain points and increase the effectiveness of trainings.

  Low-overhead Scalability

SMV is designed to be infinitely scalable. Simultaneously reach millions of learners without the enormous hassle and cost that come with growth.
